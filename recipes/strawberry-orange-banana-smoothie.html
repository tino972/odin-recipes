Strawberry Orange Banana Smoothie

Description

This orange juice smoothie with banana and strawberries is super refreshing!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • ¾ cup orange juice
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as needed

Steps

  1. Blend strawberries, orange juice, banana, and ice in a blender until smooth and frothy.
