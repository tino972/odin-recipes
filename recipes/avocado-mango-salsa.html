Avocado Mango Salsa

Description

This mango avocado salsa with habaneros is sweet, hot, and tangy! Great with chips for a summer appetizer or a side for grilled pork, chicken, or fish.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 habanero chili pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place avocado in a serving bowl and mix with lime juice.
  3. Add mango, onion, habanero, cilantro, and salt; toss until evenly combined.
  4. Enjoy!
